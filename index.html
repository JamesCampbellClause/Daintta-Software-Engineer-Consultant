The role

What you will be doing

You will work closely with public sector clients and cross-functional project teams to design, develop and implement software solutions that meet complex operational requirements.

Key responsibilities

  • Collaborate directly with government clients to understand software engineering needs and translate them into robust technical solutions
  • Design, develop, test and implement high-quality, secure and well-documented software using industry best practices
  • Architect scalable systems across multiple languages including Python, Java, C++, Rust and Go
  • Collaborate with project managers to ensure on-time, on-budget delivery across complex engagements
  • Support client pitches and presentations, actively inputting into company strategy
  • Share knowledge and stay current with the latest tooling, frameworks and security principles

Interview process

What to expect

Daintta run a structured, transparent process. James will support you at every stage, from first call to offer.

1
Intro call with James
A relaxed 20-30 minute conversation to explore the role, your background and your expectations. No technical questions at this stage.
2
First client interview
An initial conversation with the Daintta team, assessing both cultural fit and technical grounding. Typically 45-60 minutes, conducted remotely.
3
Final client interview and offer
A deeper technical and stakeholder discussion. If successful, James will manage the offer and negotiation on your behalf.
ℹ️ The stages above reflect the typical structure for this role. There is always a possibility that the process may vary slightly depending on timing or business requirements. James will keep you fully informed of any changes throughout.
